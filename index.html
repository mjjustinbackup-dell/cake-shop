Our History

SugarBliss has been a cherished part of our community since 1990, when it started as a small home bakery in a cozy kitchen. Founded by passionate bakers who believed in the magic of simple, wholesome ingredients, we've grown while staying true to our roots. Over the decades, we've delighted generations with cakes that taste like home—baked fresh for birthdays, anniversaries, and celebrations big and small.

Every cake is made from scratch using only the finest natural ingredients: real butter, fresh eggs, organic flour, and pure vanilla. No preservatives, no artificial colors or flavors—just pure bliss in every bite. Our story is one of tradition, love, and commitment to quality, passed down through years of perfecting recipes that bring joy to your table.
